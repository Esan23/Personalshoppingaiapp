# Shortlist — App Naming Analysis & Screening

**Purpose:** Document the brand-name exploration for the app (working placeholder "Shortlist"), grounded in the Problem Aware avatar, the diary entries, and the Shortlist Brand Identity & Design System. Emphasis: a name that is distinctive, ownable, and signals that **AI is core to the product**.
**Date:** June 14, 2026
**Status:** Finalists identified — pending formal trademark clearance.

> **Scope of this screening (important):** The availability reads below come from screening **apps and AI products only** (App Store, Google Play, company/AI-tool listings via web search). This is **not legal clearance.** Every finalist still requires a USPTO TESS search (Classes 9 / 35 / 42), domain + app-store + social-handle checks, and a trademark-attorney review before any brand spend.

---

## 1. Naming strategy

The AI decision/shopping-assistant category is **saturated with literal names** (decide / choose / verdict / shortlist). Competing on a literal word means fighting for an already-claimed concept and a name that is hard to trademark. The strategy is therefore:

- **Go abstract / coined**, not literal — more ownable and easier to clear.
- **Encode the brand feeling**, not just the function: calm, clarity, relief, the "three options," the loosened knot from the diary.
- **Signal AI through the lockup and positioning** (descriptor + persona framing), not by burying "AI" in the name.

**Observed pattern during screening:** every abstract candidate survived; every literal and persona candidate collided. That is the core finding driving the recommendation.

**Backups (not deeply screened — verify before use):** Vela (calm celestial guide; multiple existing tech uses), Wren (friendly persona; a carbon-removal company named Wren exists).

---

## 4. Recommendation

1. **Trine** — best meaning-to-ownability ratio. Encodes "three" + calm/harmony and is clean in-category. **Primary recommendation.**
2. **Pith** — sharpest personality and most on-brand verbally, but harder to clear as a common word.
3. **Cusp** — elegant, though "the cusp" is a softer metaphor than three-ness or essence.

### Making AI unmistakable (lockup, not name)
- **Descriptor lockup:** *Trine — your AI shopping shortlist.*
- **Persona framing** matches the diary's "ask like you'd ask a friend" voice.
- **App icon:** the brand gradient (indigo → amber, "midnight → morning") as the mark — ties name, story, and identity together.
- **Production wordmark font:** DM Serif Display (per the brand system).

---

## 5. Practical notes

- **Domains:** exact-match `.com`s for dictionary words are almost certainly taken. Plan for `trine.ai`, `usetrine.com`, or `gettrine.com`. Check the handle on App Store, Google Play, and Instagram before committing.
- **Mark concepts explored:** Trine = three connected dots in a triangle; Pith = a filled core inside a thin ring; Cusp = a crescent/turning-point arc — each reversed white on the brand gradient tile.
